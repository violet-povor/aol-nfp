About the Program

This program focuses on the healing influence of classical music on different systems in our body, allowing children to re-connect with their inner-self to reveal their natural creative abilities and prevent diseases on all levels by harmonizing body, mind and spirit without medications. Children explore their outer world through improvisation of dance, singing, drawing, performing, listening and playing music, using oratory and inventive story telling as well as collaborative and other innate skills achieved through the use of various musical instruments, visual art supplies, and everyday items such as magnets, glass, rice etc. This, in turn, can bring them unforgettable moments of self-realization and awareness. The program includes 10 one hour weekly classes.
