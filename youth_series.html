  • Youth-for-Youth Series

Our widely successful youth classical concert series has given rise to increasing participation of youth in the organization, planning, marketing and creative orchestration of youth events for the community.

Youth volunteers include musicians, designers, web developers, marketers, social media specialists and project coordinators. It's an amazing opportunity for youth to engage and experience the joy of serving in the community while being part of a team of friends, working shoulder-to-shoulder and supporting each other.

Please contact artshealhealth@gmail.com if you would like to volunteer, support or push forward an initiative for the benefit of youth and kids.
